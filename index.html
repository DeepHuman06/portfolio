About Me

I am Deepchand Gupta, a Mumbai-based actor originally from Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, and an alumnus of Bharatendu Natya Academy (BNA), Lucknow. With over 7 years of theatre experience, I have worked in a feature film and short films. My training has helped me build strong skills in character development, body movement, voice work, and performance. I am fluent in Hindi, Bhojpuri, and English, and I also have experience in dance and movement-based performance.
